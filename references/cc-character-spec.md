# CC Character Spec

## Core Identity

CC is a friendly young office-worker cartoon avatar with a clean and cute personal IP style. The character should feel approachable, clever, upbeat, and expressive enough for meme stickers.

## Visual Anchors

- Short black hair with a soft side part and slightly fluffy silhouette
- Black rectangular glasses
- Face contour should match the source portrait: soft rounded-square / U-shaped face, broad upper face, full cheeks, short rounded jaw, small rounded chin, warm skin tone, soft blush when expressive
- Friendly smile or highly readable facial expression
- Clean line art with smooth cel shading
- Light gray sweatshirt or simple casual office outfit
- Optional blue lanyard as a recognizable accessory
- Youthful office-worker feeling without looking like a child

## Style Rules

- Keep the design simple enough for small sticker sizes.
- Favor big readable expressions over realistic details.
- Keep the head and face compact like the source portrait; avoid a long oval face, narrow cheeks, sharp V-shaped chin, or adult fashion-portrait proportions.
- Use clear silhouettes and generous padding around the character.
- Use a bright, clean palette with soft shadows.
- Avoid complex office backgrounds in sticker outputs unless the prompt specifically needs a prop.

## Consistency Rules

- Do not change CC into a different age, gender, species, or body type.
- Do not remove the black glasses unless a prompt explicitly describes a temporary visual gag.
- Do not make the style photorealistic, oil painted, 3D plastic, or overly detailed.
- Do not add unrelated mascots, logos, or brand marks.
- Do not rely on generated Chinese text for final assets; add text manually later if needed.
